Experience: Online Poker Player / Strategy Analyst

Self-Employed (2015 – 2018)

Context and Motivation

During my studies, online poker became not only a source of income for me but, above all, a practical laboratory for testing statistical concepts in a real competitive environment. It was an invaluable lesson that allowed me to see how effective statistical methods and the law of large numbers are in a high-variance environment, where a disciplined, analytical approach is key.

Analytical Approach and Scale of Operation

  • Large Sample Analysis: My gameplay and strategies were tested on an analytical sample of millions of hands, which allowed for the systematic achievement of a positive expected value (EV+).
  • Real-Time Information Processing: Regularly playing on over a dozen tables simultaneously forced the development of skills for quickly processing large amounts of data (opponent statistics, game dynamics) and making optimal decisions under time pressure.
  • Practical Application of Theory: This experience involved the continuous application of statistics, game theory, and psychology to build an edge over opponents.
